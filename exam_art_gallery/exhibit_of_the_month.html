Exhibit of the Month: Starry Night by Vincent van Gogh

One of the most famous paintings in the world, Vincent van Gogh's "Starry Night" is an iconic piece of art that depicts the artist's view from the asylum of Saint-Paul-de-Mausole in Saint-Rémy-de-Provence. The swirling skies, the vibrant colors, and the emotional intensity make this piece a true masterpiece of Post-Impressionism.
